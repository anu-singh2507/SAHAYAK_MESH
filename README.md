
# sahayak_mesh
An offline disaster rescue app built with Flutter that uses Bluetooth Low Energy to detect nearby devices without the internet.

SOS Bluetooth Radar: A visual radar that scans for nearby devices and acts as a "digital whistle" to find trapped survivors.

AI Rescue Coordinator: Powered by Gemini 2.5 Flash-lite, acts as a human-like "Control" center for first aid and survival strategies.

Live Hazard Dashboard: Real-time tracking of Location, Weather, and Air Quality (AQI) with context-aware visual backgrounds (e.g., Rain/Night mode).

Tactical Survival Manual: An offline-first, interactive handbook with visual "Do's and Don'ts" for Fire, Flood, and Quake scenarios.

Disaster Prone Map : Provieds an outlook for high risk disaster prone areas.

Disaster Alerts : Provides disaster alerts near-by the situation and actions to-do.

SOS Broadcasting: One-tap activation to broadcast a high-priority distress signal.

Tactical Dark UI: High-contrast, battery-saving dark interface designed for low-light emergency conditions.
